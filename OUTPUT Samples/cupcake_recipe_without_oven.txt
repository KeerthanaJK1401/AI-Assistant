Title: Cupcake Recipe Without Oven

Cupcakes are a popular dessert enjoyed by many. However, baking them traditionally requires an oven. This article presents a unique cupcake recipe that does not require an oven, making it a convenient option for those without access to one. The recipe uses simple ingredients such as flour, baking powder, sugar, milk, and butter. The key ingredient that replaces the oven is a microwave. The process involves mixing the dry ingredients, then the wet ingredients, and finally combining the two. The mixture is then poured into microwave-safe cups and cooked in the microwave for a few minutes. The result is a moist and delicious cupcake that can be enjoyed without the need for an oven. This recipe is a great alternative for those who want to satisfy their sweet tooth without the hassle of traditional baking.
